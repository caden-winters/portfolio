About Caden Paul Winters

My name is Caden Winters and I am a public relations student at Brigham Young University. I am from Meridian Idaho, the best city, but I was originally born in San Diego California. I have always had a passion for storytelling and filmmaking, and I pursued this hobby through my teenage years. This led me to work with my friends on multiple short films and start and run my own Youtube channel. I have found that filmmaking and storytelling allows me to express myself in unique ways, and has always served as a creative outlet for me.

I have also always loved and deeply valued good communication. I have seen how problems can be solved and bridges built through understanding and connection that comes through communication done right. My hope and dream in life is to connect the world through the power of communication, and bring back a sense of trust in people's lives.
